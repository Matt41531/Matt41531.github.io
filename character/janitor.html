JAN ITOR

ROLE: TANK

DIFFICULTY:

The Clean Machine

Jan Itor is a tanky disruptor that thrives on controlling the battlefield.

Jan Itor's tankiness relies on proper ability usage. His empowered auto attack grants a shield based on the number of enemies hit and his D&D barrier will protect you from projectiles (including the tower!). Use both of these to stay alive!

Jan Itor's Movement Ability, Clean Sweap, stuns enemies that are pushed into a wall. Always be on the lookout for unique angles!

Whenever you hit more than 2 people with your ultimate, you have to yell HUUUUUUUGE TOILET.

Abilities

SQUEAKY CLEAN (P)

Janitor swings his broom wildly, dealing damage. When empowered, Jan itor's next auto attack deals additional damage, slows enemies, and grants Jan Itora shield based on the total number of enemies hit.

PLUNGER PALS (RMB)

Janitor throws a large plunger in the targeted direction that collides with the first unit hit, quickly pulling the two toward each other.

DEFEND AND DISINFECT (Q)

Janitor throws an explosion of cleaning supplies to the targeted location, creating a pristine destructible barrier that blocks enemy projectiles. Allies standing underneath the barrier are healed periodically while it is active.

CLEAN SWEEP (SB)

Jan Itor dashes in the targeted direction, knocking back enemies and dealing damage. Enemies knocked into a wall are stunned,

FINAL FLUSH (ULT)

Janitor calls forth a massive toilet that bursts from the ground after a short duration, dealing massive damage and knocking enemies in the air. The toilet acts as terrain, slowing nearby enemies.
