Hamon is a bruiser all about spinning and winning!
<br>
<br>
Hamon does not have high base tank stats, so you need to rely on lifesteal from your Q
and the shield from your SPC to survive. Going in without either of these is almost certain doom!
<br>
<br>
Hamon's ultimate can be an absolute gamechanger. Hitting multiple enemies with it can set up amazing CC
chains with allies like Jan Itor, Inksy, Hotpaws, or Gus.
